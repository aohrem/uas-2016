Help

The functionalities of the WebGIS are listed below:

  • Map

    • Search for location
    • Switch Base layers
      • Streets
      • Satellite
      • Grey Light
    • Toggle between Image Layers
      • Orthophoto
      • NDVI
      • Classification image

  • Video

    • 360 degree Video View
      • Play
      • Pause
      • Replay
      • Pann
    • Flight Marker shows the position of the drone while taking the video
    • Circular Marker shows the projection of the video. It is calculated using the angle and the altitude.
    • Drag the video to change the position of the Flight Marker

  • Export map as pdf or png
